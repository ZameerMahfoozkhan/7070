Our Journey

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Mr. Tarif Sayed, 7070 Sports (7T7T) began as a small 48 sq. ft. outlet with a bold vision: to become a leading name in India's sportswear industry. What started as a modest setup has grown into a dynamic, nationally recognized brand known for its commitment to innovation, performance, and style.

With its head office based in Mumbai, 7T7T operates a modern 8,000+ sq. ft. manufacturing facility in the Bhiwandi-Kalyan industrial zone, equipped with advanced technology and skilled craftsmanship. This strong production backbone enables us to deliver high-quality, performance-driven sportswear tailored to meet the needs of athletes and active individuals across the country.

Today, 7T7T proudly serves customers through a vast retail network of over 500 stores across India, offering accessible, durable, and stylish sportswear designed for real performance and everyday wear. In 2025, we proudly launched a new 7070 Sports franchise store in Ayodhya, further expanding our footprint and bringing our trusted products to one of India’s most iconic and rapidly growing cities

Today, we are proud to be Ayodhya's most trusted cricket equipment store, serving thousands of customers from beginners to professional players. Our commitment to quality, expertise, and customer service has made us the go-to destination for cricket gear in the region.
